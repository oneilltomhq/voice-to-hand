✅ Verdict on Your C-bet

HU vs BTN: Small c-bet with JJ is overwhelmingly correct (≥ 94% frequency across all textures). Your £30 into £105 is the right sizing.

HU vs BB: More mixed. JJ should still mostly bet (~70-75%) on rainbow/two-tone, but on a monotone board without a heart, JJ becomes a majority check (85%!).

But this is 3-way, not HU. With two opponents, the Ace hits more of their combined range, and you have less fold equity. The correct 3-way adjustment is to check more. GTO says bet in HU; multiway tips it toward check-call or possibly a very small probe.

Your intuition was partially right: "Maybe I should've checked or bet very small." In the 3-way pot, checking is more defensible than it looks. But if you do bet, the small sizing (£30 = 29%) is exactly what GTO prefers — never the 75% pot.

Monotone is the biggest check. Without a heart (JJ no heart), it's a clear check. With one heart, it's marginal.
